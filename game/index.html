    The mountain answered back

    The Kunnam Array went silent forty years ago — a research spire nine kilometres up, abandoned mid-experiment when the weather stopped obeying physics.

    Last month it started broadcasting again. One repeating signal. Your handle. Your old rope-team callsign. An invitation, or a warning — the only way to find out is up.

    Climb through three sectors of impossible terrain. Anchor your ropes, manage your heat, keep your team alive. The summit knows you're coming.
